About

Raphael Santos is a composer and programmer from Campina Grande, Brazil, where he currently studies for a bachelor's degree in music composition at UFCG. He has been a student of Liduino Pitombeira and in various festivals has attended classes and workshops with Caspar Johannes Walter, Stefano Gervasoni, Stephen David Beck e Tristan Murail.

He has worked on the fields of musical computing, algorithmic composition, stochatisc music, spectral music, mixed electroacoustic music and in the creation of novel musical instruments with electronic resources.
